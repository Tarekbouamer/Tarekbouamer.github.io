VisLoc: is a comprehensive collection of models and algorithms designed for use in Extended Reality (XR) and Robotics applications, and it consists of

  • Retrieval : it involves algorithms and models for searching and retrieving a visual data.
  • Matching : a collection of deep learning based correspondences matchers between two images.
  • Localization : suitable for image to 3D scene localization and absolute pose estimation.
