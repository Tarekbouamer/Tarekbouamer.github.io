## Abstract

VisLoc is a comprehensive collection of models and algorithms designed for use in Extended Reality (XR) and robotics applications, organized around three parts: retrieval, matching, and localization.

* [Retrieval](https://github.com/Tarekbouamer/visloc_retrieval): it involves algorithms and models for searching and retrieving a visual data.
* [Matching](https://github.com/Tarekbouamer/visloc_matching): a collection of deep learning based correspondences matchers between two images.
* [Localization](https://github.com/Tarekbouamer/visloc_retrieval): suitable for image to 3D scene localization and absolute pose estimation.
